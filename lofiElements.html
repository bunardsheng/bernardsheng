Lo-Fi Girl

Visual and Musical Immersion.

Lo-Fi girl is an aesthetically pleasing, retro-styled game that allows the player to enjoy timeless music and art in an interactive setting. Our game creates an intersection between various disciplines, highlighting positive player feedback through original soundtracks, hand-crafted pixel art, and fundamental programming.

Concept

The general concept revolves around an "evolving" map. The player begins in a dimmed-setting, hearing static as the main sound effects. Gradually, as the player progresses through their daily tasks, the map brightens, followed by the music, leading to a beautiful and timeless environment as the ultimate finale.
